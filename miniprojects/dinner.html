When We Meet

Join us every Friday at 7:00 PM! Feel free to come around 6:45 PM to mingle and get comfortable. We start on time and usually wrap up around 10:00 PM, but time flies when you're having fun!

How We Dine

Each week brings exciting flavors from different corners of the world, served family-style. The dinner features one carefully crafted dish that comes in two options: vegetarian (₵75) and non-vegetarian (₵100). Both soft drinks and alcoholic beverages are available to order from Tumi Cafe. Don't worry about dietary needs - just let us know a day ahead, and we'll make sure you're well taken care of.

The Vibe

Think of it as a dinner party with friends you haven't met yet! The atmosphere is casual and welcoming. Wear whatever makes you comfortable (though most folks go for smart casual). Love wine? Feel free to bring your favorite bottle!

Reserve Your Spot

Each Friday's menu is announced in the morning through our WhatsApp group. First-timers, please ask to join the WhatsApp group when making your reservation; it's where all the menu reveals and dinner announcements happen. Remember to book your spot before 4 PM on Friday, as that's when our reservations close. This helps us prepare the perfect amount of food and ensure everyone has a seat at the table!

Finding Us

We're at Tumi Ghana, located at Sobolo 4 junction, Asokwa. We're a few minutes drive from Bull Dog Pub, close to the Kumasi Sports stadium. Convenient parking is available on site. Find us on Google Maps: Location.
